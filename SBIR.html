SBIR Award Database Analysis

The SBIR Award Database Analysis was done during my internship at The Outpost. Data was collected from the SBIR Award Database and the descriptions for each individual award were vectorized using Open-AI's ada model and TF-IDF. These vectors then underwent dimentionality reduction through UMAP and clustering with HDBSCAN and K-Means. I then presented insights from the report to account managers, allowing them more accurately match their clients with sectors of the DoD for contracts.
